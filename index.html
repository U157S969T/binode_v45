Transforming Boots Equip into a Future-Ready Platform

Professionals rely on Boots Equip to navigate complex workflows across patient care, compliance, and retail management. As the platform grew in scope, the experience became harder to navigate and slower to use. This POV explored how Equip could evolve from a collection of tools into a cohesive, future-ready platform that supports speed, clarity, and collaboration at enterprise scale.

The Brief

Boots Opticians wanted to improve the usability of the existing Equip platform while exploring a future-state solution that could serve as a unified hub for clinical and retail workflows. We were asked to conduct a design audit and heuristic evaluation to identify experience gaps, simplify core processes, and define a scalable design direction. The focus was on experience strategy and platform vision, not immediate implementation.

The Core Concept

The core idea reframes Equip as a modular and personalized workspace rather than a static system.

Equip+ adapts to user roles, priorities, and workflows, reducing cognitive load and enabling faster decision-making. Automation and collaboration are built into the foundation, allowing teams to work more efficiently across patient records, compliance, and store management within a single ecosystem.

Key Features

Personalized Workspaces

Customizable dashboards and role-based onboarding help users focus on what matters most from day one.

Workflow Automation

Quick report generation and batch processing speed up everyday tasks, freeing up time for patient care.

The Outcome

This POV helped Boots leadership align on the need for a future-ready platform and a unified design system.

It shifted conversations from incremental fixes to long-term platform strategy and roadmap planning. The work positioned our team as a trusted partner for experience-led transformation, setting the stage for the next generation of clinical and retail tools.
